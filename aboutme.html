About Me

The kitchen is my safe space.

I remember playing Cooking Mama on my DS when I was little. I'd try to get a gold star on every dish before I'd be satisfied with my score. I didn't think my interest for that game would become a well-loved hobby of mine at this point in my life.

Most of the time, I like the control I get when it comes to the kitchen. Food almost comes close to the intuition/instinct of what I do in the kitchen, but knowing what to do without having to ask for help is a great feeling.

My culinary skills are entirely self-taught and from the Internet. I've never received any formal training, but I'd like to venture into that in the near future!

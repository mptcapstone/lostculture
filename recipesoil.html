Aglio e Olio

  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of parsley, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 2 tsp chili flakes
  • 200g of spaghetti
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Ready a pot of boiling water. Add the pasta and salt to the boiling water. Put the olive oil, garlic, and chili flakes into a skillet on low heat. Stir gently until fragrant.
  2. Cook the pasta until al dente before reserving a 1/4 cup of pasta water. Add in the reserved water, pasta, and parsley before stirring quickly. Adjust the taste with salt or pepper. Serve with more parsley or chili flakes if desired.

Pantry Pasta

  • Preparation time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking time: 12-15 minutes
  • Serves: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of basil, chopped
  • 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 35g anchovies
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 200g spaghetii
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Ready a pot of boiling water. In a skillet, add olive oil, garlic, and anchovies on medium-low heat. Stir until fragrant. Add in the cherry tomatoes before covering with a lid and reducing the heat to low. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. In the meantime, add the pasta and salt to the boiling water. Cook until al dente. Reserve a 1/4 cup of pasta water before adding the pasta, water, and basil to the sauce. Adjust to taste and garnish with more basil if desired.
